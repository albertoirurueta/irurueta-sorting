Bibliography

The algorithms implemented by this library follow the descriptions and reference C++ implementations given in Chapter 8, "Sorting and Selection", of Numerical Recipes: The Art of Scientific Computing, 3rd Edition (Cambridge University Press, 2007). Cited references

Numerical Recipes' own chapter 8 points to these two works for a deeper treatment of sorting and selection, and they are also cited directly in this library’s class-level Javadoc:

  • Knuth, D.E. 1997, Sorting and Searching, 3rd ed., vol. 3 of The Art of Computer Programming (Reading, MA: Addison-Wesley). Section 5.2.1 covers straight insertion and Shell’s method; section 5.2.3 covers Heapsort.

  • Sedgewick, R. 1998, Algorithms in C, 3rd ed. (Reading, MA: Addison-Wesley). Chapter 8 covers straight insertion and Shell’s method; Chapter 11 covers Heapsort.

  • Sedgewick, R. 1978, "Implementing Quicksort Programs", Communications of the ACM, vol. 21, pp. 847-857.

  • Press, W.H., Teukolsky, S.A., Vetterling, W.T., and Flannery, B.P. 2007, Numerical Recipes: The Art of Scientific Computing, 3rd ed. (Cambridge, UK: Cambridge University Press), Chapter 8, "Sorting and Selection", pp. 419-441.
