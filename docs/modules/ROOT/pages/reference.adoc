=== Cited references

Numerical Recipes' own chapter 8 points to these two works for a deeper treatment of sorting and
selection, and they are also cited directly in this library's class-level Javadoc:

* Knuth, D.E. 1997, _Sorting and Searching_, 3rd ed., vol. 3 of _The Art of Computer Programming_
  (Reading, MA: Addison-Wesley). Section 5.2.1 covers straight insertion and Shell's method;
  section 5.2.3 covers Heapsort.
* Sedgewick, R. 1998, _Algorithms in C_, 3rd ed. (Reading, MA: Addison-Wesley). Chapter 8 covers
  straight insertion and Shell's method; Chapter 11 covers Heapsort.
* Sedgewick, R. 1978, "Implementing Quicksort Programs", _Communications of the ACM_, vol. 21,
  pp. 847-857.
* Press, W.H., Teukolsky, S.A., Vetterling, W.T., and Flannery, B.P. 2007, _Numerical Recipes: The
  Art of Scientific Computing_, 3rd ed. (Cambridge, UK: Cambridge University Press), Chapter 8,
  "Sorting and Selection", pp. 419-441.
